Up the Elephant!

Save the Elephant from Delancey's destructive plans

Help us save the Elephant & Castle's vibrant multicultural community from developer Delancey's destructive plans. We are crowdfunding to challenge Delancey's planning consent for the scheme, We believe that Southwark has broken its own planning rules and has not taken sufficient account of the detrimental impact the redevelopment will have on minority groups.
